Details and Instructions

Calculator

Determine the greatest player of all time using statistics. Weight which categories you think are most important in a player by choosing between Scoring, Shooting, Defense, Team, and Longevity. Make the weights add to 100 and the algorithm will display who statistically is the GOAT based on your input.

Forum

Create an account and use our forum system to discuss the greatest players. Our forum system will cluster you with other users with similar preferences. The clustering groups users based on which categories they found most important in players. After 5 GOAT calculations, a user is placed into a forum group.

Recommender

Create an account and keep track of the GOATs you have calculated. Once you have calculated 5 distinct GOATs, our recommender system will generate a list of players who you might also find great. These results are based on the calculations you have input, the forum group you are in, and the other users in your group.
